Usain Bolt


The fastest man on Earth



Usain Bolt, in full Usain St. Leo Bolt, (born August 21, 1986, Montego Bay, Jamaica), Jamaican sprinter who won gold medals in the 100-metre and 200-metre races in an unprecedented three straight Olympic Games and is widely considered the greatest sprinter of all time.


There is a list of madels he won on Olympics:

  • Beijing 2008 - gold medal, he made new WR on 100m on 9,69s
  • Beijing 2008 - gold medal on 200m - 19,30s
  • London 2012 - gold medal on 100m - 9,63s
  • London 2012 - gold medal on 200m - 19,32s
  • London 2012 - gold medal 4x100m - 36,84s
  • Rio de Janeiro 2016 - gold medal on 100m - 9,81s
  • Rio de Janeiro 2016 - gold medal on 200m - 19,78s
  • Rio de Janeiro 2016 - gold medal 4x100m - 37,27s
